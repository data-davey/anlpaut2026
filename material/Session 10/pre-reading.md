# Session 10 Pre-Reading

## Theme

AI without security is a liability.

Previous sessions focused on how to build with LLMs, RAG, tools, and agents. Session 10 asks what changes when these systems are deployed in real organizations.

## Think About

Come prepared to discuss:

1. What is one AI risk that is technical rather than philosophical?
2. What private or sensitive data might a RAG system accidentally expose?
3. What extra risks appear when an AI agent can call tools?
4. What should be logged, monitored, or reviewed after an AI system goes live?
5. Why would an AI company hire a philosopher to help shape model behavior?
6. When should an organization be liable for a chatbot's answer?
7. Should an AI coding agent be allowed to publish public criticism of a human maintainer without human approval?
